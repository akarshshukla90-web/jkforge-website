Why JK Forge?

Engineered for EPCs & Refineries

JK Forge & Fittings is an established, ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturer of high-pressure piping components. We specialize in rapidly fulfilling large-scale procurement demands for EPC contractors, Petrochemical Refineries, and Oil & Gas infrastructure projects across the globe.

With advanced CNC machining and in-house forging up to Class 9000#, we guarantee 100% material traceability (MTC EN 10204 3.1) on every ASTM A105 Carbon Steel and A403 Stainless Steel fitting that leaves our Vadodara facility.
